Meet Katy

My name is Katy and I am a 3 year old 26lbs poodle. My life started off at a puppy mill where I was caged and neglected but since coming to Just Paws, I’ve learned what being loved feels like. It does take me some time to warm up to new people but once I do, I want to be by your side and watch what you are doing. I’m a low key girl in my foster home who hasn’t shown an ounce of aggression towards anyone. I haven’t had any accidents lately and I dont need a crate since I don’t put things in my mouth that I’m not suppose to. I’m looking for a family that will show me a lot of love and spend time with me. I have discovered I love going on walks and I am very polite on my leash. I also enjoy running around the yard with my dog friend Max from down the street. We have a fun time chasing each other. I do well with other dogs especially the ones that won’t jump in my face and annoy me. I would also be ok as an only dog in the home but would love some play dates. A fenced in yard would be ideal so I can bask in the sun and walk around as I please. If you think that I would fit well in your home, please apply for me now!
