Services and Facilities

  • Adoption Services: We offer a wide range of adoptable dogs, cats, and other small animals looking for loving families.

  • Spay and Neuter Clinic: We provide spaying and neutering services to help control the pet population.

  • Medical Care: Our shelter has an onsite veterinary clinic to ensure the health and well-being of our animals.

  • Education Programs: We host educational events and workshops to promote responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.

  • Volunteer Opportunities: Forever Paws Pet Haven welcomes volunteers who share our passion for animals.

Our Mission

At Forever Paws Pet Haven, our unwavering mission is to create a brighter future for homeless animals. We believe that every life matters, and our commitment to this belief drives every aspect of our work. Our mission encompasses several core principles:

  1. Rescue and Rehabilitation

    We are dedicated to rescuing animals from challenging and often dire situations. Whether they've been abandoned, neglected, or are victims of cruelty, we provide them with a lifeline, a chance for a new beginning. Through skilled care and compassionate rehabilitation, we nurse them back to physical and emotional health.

  2. Rehoming for Forever Homes

    Every animal deserves a loving, forever home. Our mission includes actively seeking and carefully selecting adoptive families who will provide safe, caring, and permanent homes. We believe in finding the perfect match for each animal to ensure a happy, lifelong companionship.

  3. Advocacy and Education

    We are not just an animal shelter; we are advocates for animal welfare. Part of our mission is to educate the community about responsible pet ownership, the importance of spaying and neutering, and the prevention of cruelty to animals. We host educational events, workshops, and outreach programs to spread awareness and knowledge.

  4. Quality Medical Care

    Our commitment to animal well-being extends to their health. Our shelter is equipped with an onsite veterinary clinic staffed by dedicated professionals. Every animal receives regular check-ups, vaccinations, and any necessary medical treatments. We believe that healthy animals are happy animals.

  5. Community and Volunteer Engagement

    We believe in the power of community and the tireless efforts of volunteers. Our mission includes fostering a strong sense of community among those who share our passion for animals. Volunteers are the heart of our organization, and we welcome individuals and groups to join us in our mission.

Our mission is a testament to our love for animals and our belief in their inherent worth. It drives us to work tirelessly, to never give up on an animal in need, and to advocate for their rights. Together, with the support of our community, we strive to create a world where every pet has a safe, loving, and forever home.
