Get to know Elliot

Introducing the cheerful Elliot, a spirited little guy who's about 6 months old. Elliot's zest for life is truly infectious, and he's got a heart full of love for everyone he meets. What makes Elliot extra special is that he doesn't let anything hold him back, not even the fact that he walks a bit differently from other dogs. His independent spirit and playful nature make him an absolute joy to be around.

Elliot is a pro when it comes to house training, pee pad training, and crate training. He's adapted to his unique situation with grace and determination. While he may not be a fan of traditional walks, he's all about running and playing in a secure, fenced yard. And don't be surprised by his agility – he effortlessly conquers any step with the help of a ramp in his foster home.

Currently residing in Pickering under the loving care of his foster family, Elliot is ready to find his forever home. If you're looking for a furry companion who embodies resilience and enthusiasm, Elliot could be the perfect match for you.
