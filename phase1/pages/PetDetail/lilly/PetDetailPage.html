Get to know Lilly

Lilly's journey began when she was separated from her mother at the tender age of 3 weeks and sold as a golden retriever puppy to a first-time dog owner. However, a visit to the vet revealed that Lilly was too young to be away from her mom and littermates, and she wasn't a golden retriever after all.

Despite the initial challenges, Lilly's story took a turn when her new owner tried to reunite her with her mother, but the breeder declined to take her back. Since then, Lilly has found her way into our care. She's now healthy, vaccinated, and ready to embark on her next chapter through adoption.

Lilly is a Maremma sheepdog, known for their large and protective nature. We're looking for a loving family committed to her training and providing her with the space she deserves. Lilly would thrive on a larger property, ideally in a country setting.

Lilly has already found a home!
